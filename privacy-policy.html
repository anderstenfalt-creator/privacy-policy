Privacy Policy – Solradar

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Solradar does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app is designed to work entirely on your device without any account, registration, or tracking.

Data We Do Not Collect

Solradar does not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information. There are no analytics, crash reporting tools, or third-party SDKs in the app.

Your Location

Solradar does not request or use your GPS location. Sun calculations are based on the map area you are currently viewing, not your physical location.

Weather Data

The app fetches publicly available weather data from SMHI (Swedish Meteorological and Hydrological Institute) based on a fixed coordinate in Gothenburg. No personal data is sent in these requests.
